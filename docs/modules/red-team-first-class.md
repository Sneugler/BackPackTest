# Red-Team and Emulation Support

AegisForge treats authorized red-team and purple-team work as first-class workflow areas through:
- scenario definitions with ATT&CK mapping,
- precondition and success-criteria tracking,
- engagement-flow checklists,
- authorized toolkit planning for safe module/tool selection based on rules of engagement,
- validation-path planning that captures entry methods, prerequisites, evidence, and guardrails for controlled exercises,
- telemetry expectation capture,
- evidence linkage and after-action reporting support,
- project governance fields for rules of engagement and authorization evidence.

The platform intentionally focuses on planning, validation, operator governance, and reporting rather than unbounded offensive automation.
